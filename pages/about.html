About Me


Hi, my name is Henry Chang. Having lived in the Lower Mainland of British Columbia for most of my life, I have had the luck and pleasure of enjoying the mountainous backyard of the North Shore. Hiking has always been a passion of mine. I love going out to explore the great outdoors at every oppurtunity. I started taking pictures of my hikes last year when I acquired a new phone (Samsung S9) and realized that I've been borne witness to so many stellar mountaintop views while hiking; yet have no record of it save for the inside of my forgetful head. I created the website Canadian Hiker as a hobby, for the sole purpose of showcasing photographs I took with my S9 camera during my hiking adventures; combining my passion for hiking with my other interest of computer programming.

This website is still a current work in progress but I hope users can still enjoy it and hopefully become inspired to go on hiking adventures of their own.
